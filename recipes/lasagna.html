Lasagna Recipe

Home

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish made by layering flat sheets of pasta with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and generous amounts of melted cheese. The meat sauce is typically made with ground beef or pork, tomatoes, onions, and herbs like basil and oregano, slow cooked until thick and flavourful. The béchamel is a simple white sauce made from butter, flour, and milk that adds a creamy richness between the layers. Everything gets stacked together in a deep baking dish, topped with mozzarella and parmesan then baked until golden and bubbling. The result is a hearty, comforting dish that tastes even better the next day.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Basil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • GarlicTomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Oregano
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and fry the onion and garlic until soft
  2. Add ground beef and cook until browned
  3. Add tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt and pepper and let it simmer for 20 minutes
  4. In a separate pan melt butter, add flour and stir, then slowly add milk to make the béchamel sauce
  5. Cook the lasagna pasta sheets in boiling salted water until al dente
  6. Preheat oven to 180°C
  7. In a deep baking dish add a layer of meat sauce, then pasta sheets, then béchamel sauce
  8. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used up
  9. Top with mozzarella and parmesan cheese
  10. Bake for 45 minutes until golden and bubbling
  11. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving
