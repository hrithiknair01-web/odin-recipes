Ice Cream

Description

Homemade ice cream is creamy, rich, and way better than anything from a shop. Made with just a few simple ingredients, it's churned into a silky smooth frozen dessert that you can customize with any flavour you love.

Ingredients

  • Heavy cream
  • Whole milk
  • Sugar
  • Egg yolks
  • Vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Whisk egg yolks and sugar together until pale and thick
  2. Heat milk in a saucepan until just simmering
  3. Slowly pour hot milk into egg mixture whisking constantly
  4. Pour everything back into the pan and cook on low heat stirring until it thickens
  5. Remove from heat and stir in heavy cream, vanilla and salt
  6. Let it cool completely then refrigerate for 2 hours
  7. Pour into a container and freeze for at least 6 hours
  8. Scoop and serve
