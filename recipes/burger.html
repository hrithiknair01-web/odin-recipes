Description

A classic beef burger is one of the most satisfying meals you can make at home. Juicy beef patties are seasoned and griddled to perfection, then stacked between soft toasted buns with melted cheese, crisp lettuce, ripe tomatoes, and tangy sauces. Simple to make but absolutely delicious every single time.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Season ground beef with salt and pepper and shape into patties
  2. Heat a pan on high heat with a little butter
  3. Cook patties for 3-4 minutes each side until browned
  4. Place cheese on top and let it melt
  5. Toast the buns in the same pan
  6. Assemble with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup and mustard
  7. Serve immediately
